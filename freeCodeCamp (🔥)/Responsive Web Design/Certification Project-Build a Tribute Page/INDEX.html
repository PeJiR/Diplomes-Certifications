History

  
Son Goku, the protagonist of the Dragon Ball anime and manga series,has captivated audiences worldwide with his endearing personality and unwavering spirit. 

Born Kakarot, a Saiyan warrior sent to Earth as an infant to conquer it,Goku's life took an unexpected turn when he was found by Gohan, a kind-hearted old man who raised him as his own son.

Despite his Saiyan heritage, Goku's upbringing instilled in him a deep-seated sense of compassion and a love for life.

He embraced martial arts training, discovering a natural talent for fighting that would lead him to become one of the most powerful warriors in the universe.

Goku's journey from a carefree boy to a seasoned hero is filled with trials and tribulations.

He has faced countless foes, each more formidable than the last, and has always emerged stronger, wiser, and more determined. 

His unwavering belief in justice and his unwavering commitment to protecting those he cares about have made him a beacon of hope for countless individuals.
Did you know?
  • Son Goku, also known as Kakarot, is the main protagonist of the Dragon Ball manga series created by Akira Toriyama.
  • He is a Saiyan, an alien race with superhuman abilities, who was sent to Earth as a child to destroy it.
  • However, Goku's head was injured as a child, causing him to lose his memory and become a kind and gentle warrior.
  • Goku spends his childhood training in martial arts and exploring the world, eventually meeting his lifelong friends Bulma, Krillin, and Yamcha.
  • Goku is known for his insatiable appetite, his love for fighting, and his unwavering determination to protect the innocent.
  • He has faced many powerful foes throughout his life, including the ruthless Saiyan Prince Vegeta, the tyrannical Emperor Frieza, and the evil android Cell.
  • Despite his many challenges, Goku has always emerged victorious, thanks to his unwavering spirit and his ability to push his limits beyond what is humanly possible.
